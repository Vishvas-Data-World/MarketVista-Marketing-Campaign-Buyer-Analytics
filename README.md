# MarketVista-Marketing-Campaign-Buyer-Analytics

## Overview
This project analyzes marketing campaigns, customer segments, and purchase drivers using Power BI.  
The goal is to help a retail business understand customer behavior and improve marketing effectiveness.

---

## Business Problem
A retail company wants to:
- Evaluate marketing campaign performance
- Understand customer demographics
- Identify purchase drivers
- Discover factors influencing total sales

## Key Insights
- Higher income customers are more likely to accept campaigns.
- Married customers form the largest customer segment.
- Older customers contribute more to wine sales.
- Household composition influences buying behavior.
